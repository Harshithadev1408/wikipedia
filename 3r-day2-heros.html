Early life and family

Kalyan was born on 2 September in 1968 or 1971[n 1] to Konidela Venkata Rao and Anjana Devi in Bapatla Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh.[23][24] He is the younger brother of Chiranjeevi and Nagendra Babu. He was awarded "Pawan" during one of his public martial arts presentations.[25] He holds a black belt in karate.[26] He is also the uncle of actors Ram Charan,[27] Varun Tej,[28] Sai Dharam Tej[29] and Allu Arjun.
